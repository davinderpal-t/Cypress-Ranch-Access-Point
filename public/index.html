Cypress Ranch Access Point

The Cypress Ranch Access Point, or CRAP is an application built by Davinderpal Toor to provide a similar experience the well-known Cyress Woods application.


The main goal of CRAP is to provide CFISD students a quick way to check their grades and test grades in order to see what they need to get a certain grade.


The mobile application will be released on the Google Play Store and currently there is no guarantee of an IOS option. If the demand is great enough there will also be a web-based version with mobile UI.
